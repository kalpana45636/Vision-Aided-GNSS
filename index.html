Understanding The Technology

What is GNSS?

Global Navigation Satellite Systems (GNSS) like GPS, Galileo, and GLONASS provide global positioning data. However, signals can be degraded by urban canyons or bad weather.

Vision Enhancement

By integrating onboard cameras and visual odometry algorithms, our system compensates for GNSS signal loss, providing continuous, high-precision localization.

Core Applications

Essential for autonomous vehicles, robotics, drone swarms, and smart public transportation where centimeter-level accuracy prevents catastrophic failures.
